Abandon all your equipment. It can hit you. 
If possible, try to shelter behind large objects.
If caught in the open, try to avoid debris by "swimming".
Be aware of dangerous terrain like cliffs and boulders.
As the snow nears you, prepare to hold your breath.
Thrust, kick and swim to stay on the surface. 
Ride on top of snow, and try to get to the snow's edge.
Keep your mouth closed, snow can fill your airways.
Bring your hands up to your face to make an air space.
